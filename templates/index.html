What is a dog meetup?

Our meetups are exactly like any other kind of meetup where you meet people with common interests.
But here it is even better, you bring your dog with you so it can make new friends too!

We created this platform to enable dog owners to meet up at dog parks or any place where dogs are accepted.
Interacting with other dogs has so many benefits!

  • They get more confident which leads to lower risk of aggressivity towards strangers and other dogs
  • They exercice while playing which keeps their mental and physical health well balanced
  • They come home relaxed and happy!

How does it work?

  • Create an account or log in if you already have one
  • Personalize your profile and tell us about your dog(s)
  • Join a meetup or create your own
  • Enjoy!
